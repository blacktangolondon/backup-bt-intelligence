Core Strategy Element: Equity Market ‘Long Bias’

The strategy is built on the historical bullish bias of equity markets. It is a long-only approach that applies a systematic “buy-the-dip” method to equities.

Markets

The system monitors stocks from the following indices:

  • FTSE 100
  • FTSE MIB
  • DAX 40
  • IBEX 35
  • S&P 500
  • NASDAQ 100

Other Information

  • When the system detects a dip that meets all required characteristics, it opens a long position with a risk-reward ratio of 1:1.
  • The holding period is capped at 60 days; any position still open at that point is closed automatically.
